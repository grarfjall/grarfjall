• Q: Why aren't there different rates for children?

A: There are several reasons for this. A large portion of the cost covers the operation of the "lifter", the vehicle that we use to break atmosphere. While children do weigh less than adults, they still require their own g-couch, and as such their lifter fee is the same. Similarly, children are alloted the same shipboard resources as adults, so their ship billing is the same.

• Q: What is the UN Orbiting Body Tax?

A: The UN Orbiting Body Tax was implemented several years ago to fund environmental programs to counter the effects of spaceflight on the local environment and local Terran space. The tax is applied to craft exiting the atmosphere, and it applies doubly to launches or craft that leave debris in Earth orbit. Fortunately, our lifters, landers, and spacecraft are all built to be reusable!
